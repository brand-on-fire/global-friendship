Our Vision

Founded in 2014, Global Friendship vision is to build meaningful connections together.

Membership Benefits

  • Feel connected to a diverse community, made up of both locals and expats.

  • Gain access to reliable and professional services for both your personal and professional development.

  • Add meaning to your life in China, by attending a variety of social, educational and community events.

Sign Up

Pillars

By working with the community, Global Friendship provides helpful, meaningful, and professional services that will support your personal and professional needs while living and working in China.

Social Events

Connect with people at a variety of social and cultural events and activities offered on a monthly basis. Take advantage of meeting people from around the world, as well as locals from various areas of China.

Educational Workshops

Personal and professional development workshops are created to provide you with the tools and resources to make improvements in your life, business or both!

Community Development

Make an impact in your community. Whether through volunteering, green initiatives or our health and wellness fair, join us to give back and connect with others in the community.

Life Services

Access an array of services and resources that will save you time and money. Whether for house cleaning, medical support, visa consultation and everything in-between. GF life services is much needed support that will make your time in China stress free.

Stats

  • Over 10,000 followers on our social media platforms

  • Diverse global network with over 100 nationalities represented

  • Over 149 events created

  • More than 97 volunteers who have participated in community development projects

ABOUT

Global Friendship (GF) is one of the largest foreign communities in southern China, with over 10,000 followers across our social media platforms. Representing more than 100 nationalities, GF truly is a global network (based in China). Locals and expatriate members recognize GF as an integral part of their community, for the support provided in either personal or business development.
